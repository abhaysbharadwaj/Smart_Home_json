to get the libraries used in this code, go to the following links and download them and place them in your arduino library folder.

you must include the "ArduinoJson", "DHT" and "SoftwareSerial"  libraries in arduino's library directory:
(C:\Program Files (x86)\Arduino\libraries)  (or ...\Arduino\libraries)

you can download the libraries from:
ArduinoJson: https://github.com/bblanchon/ArduinoJson
SoftwareSerial: https://github.com/arduino/Arduino/tree/master/hardware/arduino/avr/libraries/SoftwareSerial
DHT: https://github.com/adafruit/DHT-sensor-library
